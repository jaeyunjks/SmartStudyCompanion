## 🎓 Design Principles Applied

### 1. **Gestalt Principles**
✅ Proximity: Elements grouped logically
✅ Similarity: Consistent styling throughout
✅ Closure: Implied shapes (glass cards)
✅ Continuation: Natural flow between pages

### 2. **Color Theory**
✅ Monochromatic palette (single hue, multiple values)
✅ Complementary text colors (high contrast)
✅ Psychological impact (calming blues)
✅ Accessibility-first (colorblind-friendly)

### 3. **Typography Hierarchy**
✅ Size variation (26pt to 13pt)
✅ Weight variation (Bold to Regular)
✅ Color differentiation (dark to medium)
✅ Spacing reinforcement (clear sections)

### 4. **Motion Design**
✅ Purposeful animations (0.3s smooth easing)
✅ No jarring transitions
✅ Clear visual feedback
✅ Performance-optimized

### 5. **Accessibility**
✅ WCAG AA contrast compliance
✅ 44x44pt touch targets
✅ Clear text labels
✅ No color-only information
